Satoshi Stories

Welcome!

Satoshi stories is a ⚡lightning⚡ enabled, round based story building application that constructs stories a few words at a time. It is completely crowdsourced, that is, each story on this site is created and built upon by the collaborative efforts of the commmunity.

Contributions

Contributions can be made in a few different ways. One can:

  • Create a new story by clicking on the Create a new Story button at the top of the story list page.
  • Add a new candidate phrase to an existing story. The candidate with the most votes at the end of the round will get appended to the story.
  • Upvote or Downvote an existing candidate phrase. One vote is one satoshi.
